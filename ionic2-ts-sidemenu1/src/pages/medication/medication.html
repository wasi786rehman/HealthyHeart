TIPS TO IMPROVE ADHERENCE TO YOUR MEDICATIONS:

  • Take your medication at the same time every day.
  • If you forget to take a dose, take it as soon as you remember.
  • Do not decrease your medicine doses to save money.
  • Inform your doctor about any side-effects or other problems caused by your medicines.
  • Do not stop your medication without consulting your doctor.
  • Set medication reminder in your mobile phones
  • Involve family and friends to remind you about medications.
  • Buy your medication before you are completely out of stock.
  • When you are going out carry your medication with you so that you can take them as scheduled.
  • If you have trouble getting to the pharmacy, have financial concern, or have other problems that make it difficult to get your medication, let your doctor know.
